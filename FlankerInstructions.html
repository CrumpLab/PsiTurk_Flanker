Reaction Time task

 

Time: This task takes approximately 30-40 minutes. There are 384 trials. You will be compensated $1.50 for completing the hit.

 

Task:  Your task is to identify the letter in the center of the display. The letter will be s,d,j, or k. You will see letters appear beside the center letter. Ignore these other letters and identify the center letter by pressing the s,d,j, or k keys on the keyboard. Use both hands (left hand for s and d, right hand for j and k). Please make your responses as fast and accurately as possible.

 

Examples:  

 

you see: ssdss                     you type: d

 

Once you make a response you will automatically proceed to the next trial.

 

Every 96 trials you will receive a break screen. Feel free to take a short break.

 

This experiment requires a pop-window to be full-screen. At the end of the experiment the window will automatically close and return you to amazon where you can submit the hit.

 

Preview: You can preview this hit by continuing without accepting the hit. To complete the hit you must accept the hit before continuing. Close the practice window, then accept the hit on the main window to continue.
